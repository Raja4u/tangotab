1. What is TangoTab and how does it make money?

TangoTab is socially conscious, next generation restaurant marketing platform that provides restaurants the ability to present limited time offers to fill their empty seats when they need it most. TangoTab earns a small fee from restaurants when patrons reserve a deal through TangoTab. A portion of TangoTab's earning goes to charities that provide food for the needy on a global, national and regional basis. A person can sign up as a guest on TangoTab and reserve deals for free. They pay the restaurant directly when they visit a restaurant and use the deal they reserved!

2. Can for-profit organizations participate?

Absolutely! Any organization that wants to raise funds can participate. These organizations become affiliates of TangoTab and can take pride in the fact that TangoTab is a socially conscious entity that gives to many charities.


3. Do we have to buy anything?

No. You do not have to buy anything nor do you need to provide your credit or debit card to hold against any inventory or products. You simply have to sign up at zero cost and start enjoying discounted meals at restaurants.


4. How does it work?

i. Sign up by clicking here and completing the form to become a TangoTab Fundraiser and receive your own referral website link (URL).
ii. Provide this site to your ambassadors (parents, colleagues, friends and family) as part of your fundraising campaign so they can sign up and recruit others to sign up.
iii. When a visitor signs up using this URL, your referral code is attached to it and your earnings are allocated to your account.
iv. This visitor is now associated with you and every time they redeem a deal through TangoTab, you get credited with the earnings listed on your fundraising chart.

Once you sign up, you are now an affiliate. To read our Affiliate Agreement, click here.

To reach out to a wider audience, you can also place a banner that we will provide you with on your website. This banner links your current website to your specially developed TangoTab fundraising site. Your referral code is attached to the banner that takes the visitor to your fundraising page to sign up. This code helps keep track of people and organizations that you refer.

6. How do we know how much money we have made?

We send you a report with your check at the end of each quarter. You should also have your ambassadors provide you with the names of their friends and colleagues that have signed up at the end of each month and post the best performers on your bulletin board and in your newsletters. This will help motivate your ambassadors to work hard for you to continually earn more.

7. When and how do we get paid?

On the 10th day following the end of each quarter, we will email you your fundraising report and issue you a check for all visitors who signed up and redeemed deals.

8. What do I need to do to get started?

All you have to do is "Sign Up". We will provide you with a unique link to give to your ambassadors to forward to their friends, family, and colleagues.

10. What tools do I get to manage the fundraiser?

As part of your campaign, you will receive a unique web link to provide to your ambassadors, sample verbiage for your referral email, and sample posts for your Facebook and twitter pages.
