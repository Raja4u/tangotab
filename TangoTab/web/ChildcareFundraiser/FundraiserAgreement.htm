Agreement:

 

I agree to the following Fundraising Agreement (“Agreement”) between TANGOTAB, INC, a Delaware corporation (“Tangotab”) with offices in Plano, Texas and us (“Fundraising Entity”) (individually, “Party” and collectively the “Parties”). In consideration of the mutual agreements contained herein and intending to be legally bound hereby, Tangotab and Fundraising Entity hereby agree to all of the following terms and conditions:

 

1.             Relationship of the Parties – Fundraising Entity’s relationship to Tangotab is that of an independent contractor. Fundraising Entity is not an agent or employee of Tangotab. Fundraising Entity shall have no authority to bind Tangotab. Fundraising Entity shall promote Tangotab’s services to Entity’s customers and/or members.

2.             Commission Payments

2.1           Commissions. Tangotab shall pay to Fundraising Entity commissions calculated as follows:

(a)           Eligible customers and/or members referred to Tangotab by Fundraising Entity will be identified through a unique URL provided by Tangotab for the Fundraising Entity to use with its customers and/or members.

(b)           Fundraising Entity will receive 5% of reservation transaction fee in commission for each individual registered and 6.25% of reservation transaction fee in commission for reservations made by individual (as identified in item #2.1 (a) above) for one full year from time of registration.

(c)            Revenues, as used in item 2.1(b) above, is defined as:

(i)             Gross revenues less Sales Taxes, Refunds, Invalidated Credit Card Transactions and Credit Card Processing Fees as applicable.

(1)           Sales Taxes shall include all applicable federal, state and local sales taxes payable imposed on the restaurant reservations made by the customers and/or members of the Fundraising Entity;

(2)           Refunds shall include all revenues credited to the customers and/or members of the Fundraising Entity;

(3)           Invalidated Credit Card Transactions include, but are not limited to: (a) credit card transactions invalidated due to fraudulent card use or over-limit accounts, and (b) credit card transactions invalidated by charge-backs;

(4)           Credit Card Processing Fees include, but are not limited to, the credit card fees, charge-back fees and other bank related order processing fees.

(d)           All commissions due per this Agreement shall be payable only for as long as this Agreement is in full force and affect and has not been terminated by either party.   

2.2           Commission Statements. Tangotab shall provide Fundraising Entity with quarterly commission statements.

2.3           Payment Terms. Tangotab shall pay all commissions due to Fundraising Entity by the 10th day following the end of each quarter.  

2.4           Expenses. The Fundraising Entity shall be solely responsible for the expenses incurred by Fundraising Entity in the promotion of Tangotab’s services to the Fundraising Entity’s customers and/or members.

 

3.             Eligible  Referrals

3.1           To be eligible for commissions, the customers must be over the age of 18 and live within the US or Canada. 

3.2           Commissions for referrals will not be paid to Fundraising Entity where the information regarding the referral was received previously by Tangotab through a source independent of the Fundraising Entity.

3.3           If the Fundraising Entity disputes Tangotab’s receipt of the referral from another source prior to Fundraising Entity’s referral, Tangotab’s VP of Sales will resolve all such disputes. Fundraising Entity agrees that the decision of the VP of Sales will be final and binding on the Fundraising Entity and may not be appealed or disputed in any other forum.

(a)           is known to the receiving party before receipt from the other party, as substantiated by cogent and reliable evidence;

(b)           is disclosed to the receiving party in good faith by a third party who had a right to make such disclosure;

(c)            is made public by the originating party, or is established to be a part of the public domain otherwise than as a consequence of a breach by the receiving party of its obligations hereunder; or

(d)           is required to be disclosed by law or by a valid order of any governmental body or competent securities regulatory authority, provided that the receiving party shall give the disclosing party reasonable notice of such disclosure and shall take all reasonable steps available to maintain the information in confidence.

4.2           Limited Use. All Confidential Information of each party shall be used by the other party strictly and only for the purposes in this Agreement.

4.3           Reasonable Care. Each party shall have a fiduciary duty to hold all Confidential Information of the other party in confidence strictly for, and on behalf of the other party.

4.4           Obligations of the parties. Each party shall have an obligation commensurate with the duty of care set out in this Article to prevent the other party's Confidential Information in its possession control from being misappropriated or wrongfully communicated by any business partner, employee, consultant or other person under the obliged party's control.

5.             Term and Termination

5.1           Term. The initial term of this Agreement shall be for a period of one (1) year commencing from the Effective Date and thereafter this Agreement shall automatically renew for additional one (1) year periods unless either Party provides the other Party with written notice of termination at least 30 days prior to the expiration of the then current term and this Agreement has not been terminated prior to the renewal date for reasons set out below.

5.2           Termination in the Ordinary Course. This Agreement will terminate in the ordinary course upon the expiration of the last term. Thereafter, neither Party shall have any further obligation to the other under this Agreement except for those obligations, which are expressly stated to survive.

5.3           Termination for Cause. Either party will have the right to immediately terminate this Agreement if:

(a)           the other party breaches any material obligation under this Agreement and such breach has continued un-remedied for a period of ten (10) days after receiving written notice of the breach.

 

5.4           Termination by Tangotab. This Agreement may be terminated immediately by Tangotab if:

(a)           Fundraising Entity refuses to comply with any reasonable instruction, whether written or oral, of Tangotab or its officers within the earlier of (i) thirty (30) days of the date of such order or instruction even after the opportunity to comply; or (ii) such shorter period of time that is reasonable considering the particular circumstances relating to the making of such instruction;

(b)           Fundraising Entity is convicted of any criminal offence;

(c)            At any time at Tangotab’s sole discretion.
